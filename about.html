Right in the Heart of Good Times

Welcome to Midtown.

Midtown is your laid-back local hangout where good beer, great food, and even better company come together. Whether you're craving crispy wings, a slice of pizza, or just a cold one after work, we've got you covered.

Grab a seat on our open-air patio overlooking Main Street, challenge your friends to shuffleboard, or let nostalgia kick in at the classic arcade machine. From solo sips to family dinners, Midtown is where the neighborhood meets for a good time.

We opened in 2025 with one promise: Fresh. Different. Better. That means hand-stretched dough, scratch-made sauces, real pulled pork and pork belly, a salad bar that's loaded daily, and pours of cold beer and house cocktails you'd actually order twice.

We're proud to call 222 North Kansas Avenue home — right in the heart of Liberal, right in the heart of good times.
